Book reviews

Short, practical notes on books I’ve read.

Rating key

  • ★★★ – Highly recommended
  • ★★ – Recommended
  • ★ – Recommended only if interested in the topic

Books not scoring >1 star have not been reviewed

There is no anti memetics division
qntm · Genre: Fiction: Sci-Fi
Rating: ★★

Description:
Good fun, lots of original concepts. Audible version is recommended as the voice / sound effects are fantastic.

A World Undone: The Story of the Great War 1914 to 1918
G J Meyer · Genre: History
Rating: ★★★

Description:
Outstanding, best book on world war 1 in recent years. Really brings the events, characters, and storyline to life. Appropriate level of background information to understand the key events. Stylistically very well written.

Barbarians at the Gate: The Fall of RJR Nabisco
Bryan Burrough · Genre: History - Finance
Rating: ★★★

Description:
Business books (historical) are generally hard to write well given there are limitations to the depth of the story and the characters. However, this one was superbly written and gives a lot to somebody both with or without prior PE/finance experience
